🖐️ Speech Controlled Chess Game

A chess game controlled by voice commands!

Say a command and end with "confirm"...

Available commands:

  • "Start game confirm" – start the game & timer
  • "Clear board confirm" – clear all pieces
  • "Pause confirm" – pause the timer
  • "Resume confirm" – resume the timer
  • "Reset confirm" – reset the game & timer
  • "E2 to E4 confirm" – move a piece (say "confirm" to finish)

Say "confirm" at the end of your command to stop recording
